Room, conference ticket, food, coffee, drinks and PyLatam swag.

You are not gonna want to sit this one out

The amount of perks we were able to put together under one price wasn't easy, but we're super please with the result. We've put a list below of all the bells and whistles that this awesome conference includes for what you'll pay.

 

All-Inclusive Pricing Gives You

 

In Room Terrace or balcony

Enjoy outdoor access from the comfort of your room.

 

Free Food & Beverages

All you can eat 24h food and drinks, buffet or a la carte.

 

Free Parking

Valet and ample parking available for groups of all sizes 

 

Fitness Center

Fully equiped fitness center open from 7am to 10pm.

 

Spa

Beautiful space with Sauna and Jacuzzi. Massage services not included.

 

Water Sports

Kayak and Paddle boards available. Just call to schedule.

 

Tennis Court

Open tennis court.

 

Free WiFi

Coverage across the hotel.

 

Kids Club

Kids activities start at 11am and end at 5pm.

 

Themed Nights and/or Shows

Starting around 8pm every night.
