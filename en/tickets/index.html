Hotel Reservation 

How do I make my reservation at Friendly Vallarta?

Once logged in to pylatam.org, go to the ticket section on the board. From there click on "get your ticket now" and follow the instructions. This will take you to the Friendly Vallarta website. Once the reservation is complete, return to the board to confirm your reservation.

A reservation can be shared by several people, up to the number of beds available in the room. To do this all you need to do is share or ask for a reservation number to confirm each registration.


3 Easy steps

  1. Sign up at pylatam.org
  2. Make your hotel reservation
  3. Confirm your reservation number on the board

 

If you need help check the instructions  👇

Detailed Instructions

 

1. Sign up at pylatam.org ▾

Step 1. Sign up for an account

Go to the registration page and fill out the form

 

Step 2. Confirm your account

After filling out the form you will receive a confirmation email to the email you used. Click on the confirmation link and you will be redirected to the PyLatam site to log in.
 

Step 3. Login

The login button is located in the upper right corner or in the Login link. Enter your personal details and click "Login". You will be redirected to a page to manage your account.

2. Make your hotel reservation ▾

 

Step 1. Login.

 

Step 2. Make a reservation.

In the second column of the board called Tickets, click on "Get your ticket now"..

Select the day of arrival and the day of departure from the hotel. Also the number of people in the room. 


Click on "Go to Friendly Vallarta"

You will be redirected to the hotel page to make the reservation. The new page has the data you selected previously and you only have to click on "Book Now".

 

3. Confirm your reservation number on the board ▾

Step 1. Confirm your reservation.


When you complete the procedure with the hotel you will obtain a confirmation number and then you must return to PyLatam and complete your registration.
On the administration board, in the Ticket column, click on "Confirm your reservation".

On the next screen you can enter your name, email and confirmation number.

Automatically the platform connects to the hotel to check that the data matches and the dashboard will change with your booking information.
