IoT Adventures with Python


by Fernanda Ochoa

Sala: Nautilus
Día: 30 de Agosto de 2019
Hora: 15:40
Idioma: English

Biografía del ponente

Abstracto

Have you ever wondered how to control the world around us through a Python script? I will share the wonderful and interesting process of how we can integrate python with other libraries, some basic circuits and serverless platforms to have control and automate our daily life, yes REAL LIFE.

Descripción

Nowadays the Internet of Things is a trend, necessity and perhaps the most complex thing to work with due to the incredible amount of technologies involved. In this talk we will learn how to connect, program and control any electrical device that we have at hand.

We'll learn what kind of board we need depending on the project to develop and thanks to Python the programming will be a few lines of code.

Join me in this adventure in the world of Python, the Internet and his Things.
